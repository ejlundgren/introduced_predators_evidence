Desert bettong

Bettongia anhydra

Blamed on cats

IUCN Status: Extinct

Last Seen: Bettongia anhydra were last seen in 1933 in McEwin Hills, Lake Mackay, western Northern Territory

Studies in support

Desert bettongs were last confirmed 48 years after cats arrived (Wallach et al.202X).

Studies not in support

No studies

Is the threat claim evidence-based?

There are no studies evidencing a link between cats and the extinction of desert bettongs.
