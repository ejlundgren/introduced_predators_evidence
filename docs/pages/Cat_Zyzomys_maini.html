Arnhem Land rock rat

Zyzomys maini

Blamed on cats

IUCN Status: Vulnerable

EPBC Threat Rating: High/Very High

IUCN Claim: ‘Predation by feral cats may also be a threat.’’

Studies in support

Rock rats among small mammals negatively correlated with cat occupancy (Stobo-Wilson et al. 2020a).

Studies not in support

No studies

Is the threat claim evidence-based?

Cats have been documented among a range of ecological variables negatively correlated with Arnhem Land rock rats. Causality cannot be inferred due to confounding variables.
