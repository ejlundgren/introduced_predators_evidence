Lesser bilby

Macrotis leucura

Blamed on cats

IUCN Status: Extinct

Last Seen: Macrotis leucura were last seen in 1967 in Alice Springs near the edge of the Simpson Desert

Studies in support

Lesser bilbies were last confirmed 56 and 92 years after cats arrived (Wallach et al.202X).

Studies not in support

No studies

Is the threat claim evidence-based?

There are no studies evidencing a link between cats and the extinction of lesser bilbies. The two species co-occurred for over half-a-century.
