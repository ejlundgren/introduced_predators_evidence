Julia Creek dunnart

Sminthopsis douglasi

IUCN Status: Near Threatened

EPBC Threat Rating: Very high

IUCN Claim: Exotic predators (especially cats and foxes)

Studies in support

Cats hunt them (Kutt 2003; Kutt 2011; Kutt 2012; Mifsud & Woolley 2012).

Studies not in support

Cats do not select for Smithopsis dunnarts (Kutt 2012).

Is the threat claim evidence-based?

There are no studies linking cats to Julia Creek dunnart populations.
