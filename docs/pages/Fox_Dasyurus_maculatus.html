Tiger quoll

Dasyurus maculatus

IUCN Status: Near Threatened

EPBC Threat Rating: Very high

IUCN Claim: Predation by foxes and dogs

Studies in support

A fox consumed part of a quoll, possibly scavenged (Krtner et al.2003).

Studies not in support

Krtner et al.(2003) found no effect of poison baiting on quoll abundance and no effect on fox abundance in 3 of 4 sites or years.

Is the threat claim evidence-based?

There are no studies linking foxes to tiger quoll populations.
